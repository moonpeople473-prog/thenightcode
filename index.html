Fraud Prevention Tips


  • Never share your personal information over unsolicited calls or emails.
  • Use strong, unique passwords and enable two-factor authentication.
    [Our team has created a site where you can generate unique passwords up to 64 digits.]
  • Verify the source before clicking on any links or downloading attachments.
  • Keep your software and antivirus up to date.
  • Monitor your financial statements regularly for suspicious activity.
